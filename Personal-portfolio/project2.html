Project 2

Organic evolution (2021)

Description:

In a hyper-technological era, a virus threat emerges. The principle of a technique that dominates the world suddenly faces the challenge of an unpredictable and increasingly powerful nature of humanity. The idea of my project is to create sculptures with alien and menacing organic forms, such as viruses, tentacular shapes, etc.

Type:

Rendering for Land Art project

Implementation:

The project involves creating 2 or 3 elements with variable heights of 4-5 meters, made of plastic materials.

Placement:

Temporary; outdoors; located in urban spaces such as squares and mall shop exterior.

Artist Inspiration:

Organic sculpture. Christo.
