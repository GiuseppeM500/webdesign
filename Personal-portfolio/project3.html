Project 3

Vietato Attraversare (2018)
( Forbidden to Cross)

Description:

An unlikely robot encounters a road to cross. On the other side of the road, there's a sign indicating "forbidden to cross," and cars are speeding by at high speeds. Will our robot be able to cross the road?

Type:

3D animation short

Implementation:

Animation in 3D using only Blender software.

Placement:

Short for 3D Animation courses in Accademia di Belle Arti di Catania, Italy

Artist Inspiration:

Pixar, Disney
